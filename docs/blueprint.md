# **App Name**: Cylindera

## Core Features:

- Customer Profile Matrix: Comprehensive customer management allowing for profile creation, editing, and detailed contact storage with custom notes.
- Bi-Calendar Transaction Engine: LPG In/Out recording with support for Bikram Sambat (BS) and Gregorian (AD) calendars, featuring a specialized Nepali date picker.
- Auto-Balance Ledger: Real-time calculation of cylinder balances showing 'To Receive', 'To Give', or 'Settled' status automatically after every entry.
- AI Insight Reporter: Generative AI tool that summarizes customer transaction behavior and identifies risk levels for outstanding balances to assist in business decisions.
- Statement Hub: One-click generation of professional PDF statements and integrated WhatsApp sharing for balances and transaction history.
- Logistics Dashboard: High-level visual summary of total cylinders in/out, monthly volume, and filtered lists of high-priority outstanding customers.
- Data Core: Centralized Firestore database to securely manage and store customer records, transaction history, and business logic state.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Golden Amber (#E5AF34), representing high-value utility and reliability. Background: Deep Carbon (#1D1B16), providing a dark, high-contrast workspace. Accent: Ember Orange (#E34A2E) for negative balances and critical action points.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for headlines and digital labels to convey a modern, technical feel; 'Inter' for the main body text and numerical ledgers to ensure maximum legibility.
- Line-art icons with consistent 2px stroke weight, utilizing high-contrast industrial symbols for cylinder management.
- A sidebar-focused navigation structure to allow quick switching between the Dashboard, Customer Search, and Global Transactions.
- Micro-interactions on balance updates, where numbers count up or down subtly to signify automatic logic completion.
